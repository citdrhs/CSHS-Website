Computer Science Honor Society

As stated in the Virginia Computer Science Honor Society Constitution, the Deep Run Computer Science Honor Society aims to create enthusiasm for computer science and recognize academic excellence for the Computer Science disciplines. Through service projects and active tutoring, we hope to better our school and community.

Join CSHS

The Deep Run Chapter of CSHS inducts new members annually in October.

Requirements to Join

Must have taken APCSP or APCSA and maintaned a B+ (87.0)
Must have a good academic standing
Be dedicated and interested in Computer Science

How to Join

In order to be eligible for induction, you must fill out the application, which will be made available in either September or October See Ms. Norris or any of our officers for help or information about the application process, and any questions about CSHS.
