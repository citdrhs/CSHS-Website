Information about Points


As a CSHS Member, you must earn a minimum of 10 points per year, with at least 2.5 coming from meetings

You can earn points from attending meetings, completing service opportunities, or through participation

In total, there are 8 meetings worth 0.5 points each. If you miss a meeting, you can recieve 0.25 meeting points by watching an edpuzzle.

In order to log points, use the Logging Points form to the right, and your points will be updated once they are approved.

You can check your points below using our Point Viewing System

Points from previous years roll over.

Contact our Secretary, Heena Parekh, at hcps-parekhhp@henricostudents.org if you have any questions about points.
